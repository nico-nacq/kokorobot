Old old art featuring some of the Circa characters. Many are characters I've had forever... they've changed a lot over the years. This art was made between the years 2004 and 2010. I was a big fan of Digimon at the time and so my characters were based loosely on that universe.

The story was centered around Esse and Sy back then, then Esse and a few other characters were phased out, and I focused on Sy, Token, Orion and Kim (my friend's character). In all, the story was very dramatic, and very violent. The digital world had become a parallel universe, acting as a sort of limbo between the living world and the dead world. The 'digimon' of my characters became creatures living in "limbo", and their enemies were demons that had escaped the dead world. My characters had very human problems too, with authorities and each other.

Warning. Some of the art features sensitive material like substance addiction, firearms, death, suicide, blood and gore.

View at your own risk.
