Dante

  • Age: irrelevant

  • Gender: Cis gender male

  • Appearance: Mid-length black hair. He is short for a near pure blood, with an athletic build.

Dante is a Hegata but doesn't live with the others, never has. He shows himself to few and speaks little of himself.

Dante is nimble, making an enemy of him is a dangerous thing. He fears neither pain nor death, most will run at the sight of him.
