Thousand rooms

Thousand Rooms is a visual novel collaboration with Devine Lu Linvega, following the behaviours of four characters and a room.

We have written a book in the style of Borges for children, in which the reader follows a bat, a cat, an owl and a fox who try and make sense of a simple system.

The book is available in 13 languages, and can thus be used as a learning aid. All languares are available upon purchase.
