Rek Bellum

Cartoonist and sailor.

  • Answers to they/them/theirs.
  • Writes eco fiction.
  • Draws comics.
  • Lives on a sailboat.
  • Loves plants.
  • Enjoys cooking.

I am Rek, a non-binary artist currently based in the waters of the Pacific Northwest where the weather is mild and kinder to sailboats, but my heart is in Québec.

I enjoy drawing by hand with ink, and am fond of long ocean passages, food preservation, cycling and birds (do ask me about cormorants). I make art for magazines, children's books, as well as visuals for software and games. My first self-published game Oquonie (designed with Devine) was nominated for a visual arts award at the 2015 Independent Games Festival in San Francisco.

I've been making websites since I was a teenager, I would farm free geocities space by hot-linking between mutiple accounts. I've since abandoned that practice. These days, I enjoy making and maintaining simple websites. I manage GrimGrains, 100r.co, Wiktopher and Circa Comic.

Email me at rekkabell @ gmail dot com for art-related inquiries. You can also find me on Mastodon. Keep up with updates to this site with RSS.
