Couples and Loved Ones illustration

Over the years I've gotten commission requests for couples/loved ones, for weddings, birthdays or other. These are the ones I've had requested so far.

I take requests, just contact me by email at rekkabell at gmail dot com. I do both color, and lined drawings. For a two person, full-color portrait price ranges from 180$ to 250$CAD. Line drawings range from 100$ to 150$CAD.
