Couples and Loved Ones illustration

Over the years I've gotten commission requests for couples/loved ones, for weddings, birthdays or other. These are the ones I've had requested so far.

I take requests, just contact me by email at rekkabell at gmail dot com. I do both color, and lined drawings.

Full-color portraits range from $250 - $500 CAD(higher price for more characters, or accessories). Line drawings range from $150 - $300 CAD.
