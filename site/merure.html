What is Merure?

Merure is a comic anthology published by Drownspire, a non-profit collective created by myself and Devine. The collective published 3 full-colour volumes from 2008 to 2010, with short comics by 10-15 artists from Montreal. The comics below are the ones I made that were included in the first two volumes. The physical volumes are now out of print.

Published in Merure vol. I, it's the story of a girl who lives outside of time.
