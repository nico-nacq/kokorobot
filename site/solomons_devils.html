The Solomon's Devils are a group of activists whose goal is to protect the Taiga by way of disrupting any new development, or logging operations in the area.

Most of the group camp out in the Taiga full time, at a camp nicknamed Hemlock. Hemlock isn't locked to as single place, it moves constantly to keep its occupants safe.

The Solomon's Devils was started by yegon and kaddali. Its current members are: dae, yenifer, and anuk.
