Busy Doing Nothing

Busy Doing Nothing is a 217-page e-book, chronicling daily happenings on the 51-day passage of HundredRabbits from Japan to Canada.

The original logbook was transcribed, and released online in August 2020. On February 17th 2021, Hundredrabbits released a formatted revised edition for e-readers with extra content.
