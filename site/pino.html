Papa India November Oskar

Pino, Pino, Pino yotto no ko! Aoi umi kara, yatte kita!

  • Size: 10 m
  • Type: Yamaha33
  • Year: 1982
  • Material: Fibreglass
  • Countries visited: Mexico, French Polynesia, Cook Islands, Tonga, Niue, New Zealand, Marshall Islands and Japan
  • Distance covered: 36'661 km

Pino has been home to me and my partner Devine since 2016. We have traveled around the south and north Pacific together, and experienced many great and difficult things.

My sailboat is a Japanese boat. It was manufactured in Shizuoka and put together in the Pacific Northwest. It is a simple boat, with simple systems. Pino has a wood stove for heating, no refrigeration, no hot water or water pressure, and a minimum of high-power devices.
