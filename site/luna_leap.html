Lun-di

  • Story: medussas four
  • Racing name: Luna Leap
  • Family: Lepori
  • Gender: Aporagender, uses xe/xem/xyrs pronouns
  • Appearance: Long ears, green-ish skin with light patterns, strong build, dark eyes.
  • Propulsion: Cyclomedussa named ChaCha

No one else can tame and train cyclomedussas, these rare soft-bodied creatures of speed. ChaCha is friend to Lun-di, they're perfectly attuned to each other's thoughts and feelings. ChaCha's underside has tentacles that twist and turn with incredible speed, pushing the air to propel itself forward.

As a young Lepori, Lun-di developed an interest for racing because of Encela, grandparent and racer extraordinaire. Xe developed a great dislike for the IFS (interplanetary federation of ships), because of their affluence and rigidity. After Encela was forcibly removed from the Belt Championship for using a non-authorized style of ship, Lun-di set out to form xem own league, a group of racers dedicated not to form or rules, but to speed and accessibility.
