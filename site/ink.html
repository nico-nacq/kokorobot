Original art

Once in a while, I illustrate and sell original ink drawings. The subjects vary, but often include plants. Below is a list of my current series:

  • Fungi
  • Knots
  • Ginger roots
  • Fasciation

Drawing sizes vary. They are drawn on 300 gsm watercolour paper, and sold for 30-50$, depending on the complexity of the design (+ 5-10$ for shipping).
