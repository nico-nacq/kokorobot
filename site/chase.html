Chase

Context. Dae spends most of his time in the Taiga, sabotaging human operations, and running messages between The Ars Goetia and the Solo Devs. Many human hostiles go to camp out in the woods with the goal to hunt him, and the Solo Devs down. On one of his runs, he stumbles on a camp with heavily armed humans. Dae is worried, and decides to go and take a closer look. Dae is known as the ToT(Terror of the Taiga), he was given this name because of how good he is at crippling local logging operations.
