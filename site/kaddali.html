Kaddali

  • Alignment: Lawful Good
  • Other names: Kidd
  • Gender: Non-binary
  • Orientation: Pansexual
  • Appearance: Tall, blue-haired, red-eyed, pale-skinned.

Kaddali is the current acting leader of the Solomon's Devils(Solo Devs). They use the name Kidd to help mask their identity.

They come from a small community that tattoos the noses of members black when they come of age. Kaddali was estranged from that community because many did not care for their extreme activism.

Kaddali is highly attuned to the emotions of others, but also has trouble regulating their own.
