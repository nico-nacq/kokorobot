Oquonie

Oquonie (2014) is a textless puzzle game created in collaboration with Devine Lu Linvega where you must find your way across an intertwined megastructure. You will not be alone, your incarnations will have the help of bizaroid characters speaking an obscure language.

Published by Hundred Rabbits. Visit the microsite
