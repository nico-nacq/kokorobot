Sy Keon-Cross

  • Story: Circa
  • Age: 21
  • Alignment: Chaotic evil
  • Gender: Cis male
  • Orientation: Cis male
  • Family: Tim Keon, Harlow Cross
  • Appearance: Dark hair. Face stuck in a state of perpetual anger, with eyebrows permanently arched over a pair of blue eyes. Mid-height, skinny but with a strong build. Tattoo of an inverted cross and serpent on the right shoulder. Wears loose t-shirts, old jeans, and tattered converse; often wears a cap.

Sy is known in the community as a troublemaker, a person you just don't cross (ha). He grew up with few rules, and little direction, because of this he's always lived on his own terms.

He has an explosive temper, it doesn't take much to set him off. He is callous, but honest and doesn't lie. Sy's lack of foresight never fails to get him into trouble, and because he fears little, he will boldly stand in the face of a menacing character. In general, Sy is a pretty tempestuous guy, but he also loves life, and in his own fatalistic way he remains an optimist.
