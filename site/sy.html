Sy Keon-Cross

  • Story: Circa
  • Age: 21
  • Gang: Tusky Bruisers
  • Alignment: Chaotic evil
  • Gender: Cis male
  • Orientation: Cis male
  • Family: Tim Keon & Harlow Cross
  • Appearance: Dark hair. Face stuck in a state of perpetual anger, with eyebrows permanently arched over blue eyes. Mid-height, skinny but with a strong build. Tattoo of an inverted cross and serpent on the right shoulder. Wears loose t-shirts, old jeans, and tattered converse; often wears a cap.

Sy is known in the community, and gets along well enough with those in Tusky but not with anyone outside of this circle. Sy is often mixed in turf wars, between his city and those that share its border.

Sy grew up with few rules, and little direction. Because of this he's always lived on his own terms.

He has an explosive temper, it doesn't take much to set him off. He is callous, but honest and doesn't like to lie. Sy's lack of foresight never fails to get him into trouble, and because he fears little, he will boldly stand in the face of a menacing character.
