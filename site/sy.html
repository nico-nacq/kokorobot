Sy Keon-Cross

  • Story: hakum
  • Alignment: Chaotic evil
  • Gender: Cis male
  • Orientation: Heterosexual
  • Family: Tim Keon, Harlow Cross
  • Appearance: Dark hair. Face stuck in a state of perpetual anger, with eyebrows permanently arched over a pair of blue eyes. Mid-height, skinny but with a strong build. Tattoo of an inverted cross and backward S on the left shoulder, a simplified version of the 9 circles hell graphic from Dante Alighieri's Inferno on his left shoulder, and flames that start at the wrist and end halfway up his forearms. Wears loose t-shirts, old jeans, and tattered converse; often wears a cap.

Sy is a human, known as a troublemaker, a person you just don't cross (ha). He grew up with few rules, and little direction, because of this he's always lived on his own terms.

He has an explosive temper, it doesn't take much to set him off. He is callous, but honest and doesn't lie. Sy's lack of foresight never fails to get him into trouble, and because he fears little, he will boldly stand in the face of a menacing character. In general, Sy is a pretty tempestuous guy, but he also loves life, and in his own fatalistic way he remains an optimist.

kim is the only person keeping him from a life of crime, helping him to redirect his energy towards more productive goals. Sy is now a bruiser for both the ars goetia, and the solomons devils.
