Cee

  • Story: mindbird
  • Appearance: Cee is a skeleton, her skull has a large crack in it.
  • Relation: Friend of shiba killy, host of viktor

Cee is a jittery character, plagued and paralyzed by fear and anxiety, due to the precense of a bird in her head. viktor, the bird, took up residence in Cee's head early in her life, and cannot be persuaded to leave.

Cee likes to grow plants, and spends a lot of time alone or with shiba killy, her most trusted friend. Another comes to nest into her head on occasion, an immortal mouse named finn.
