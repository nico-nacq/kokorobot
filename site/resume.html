Skills

An illustrator by trade, I have illustrated many children’s books for edition companies such as Éditions Druide, ERPI and Bayard Canada. I have illustrated content for iphone and desktop games, one of these games was a finalist for “Excellence in visual art” at the IGF awards. I am also an avid cook, and sailor.
