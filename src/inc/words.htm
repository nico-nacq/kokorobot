• Mondegreen — a mishearing or misinterpretation of a phrase in a way that gives it a new meaning.
  • Malapropism — the mistaken use of an incorrect word in place of a word with a similar sound, resulting in a nonsensical, sometimes humorous utterance.
  • Spoonerism — an error in speech in which corresponding consonants, vowels, or morphemes are switched (see metathesis) between two words in a phrase.
