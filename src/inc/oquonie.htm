Oquonie

Oquonie (2014) is a textless puzzle game for PC, Linux, Mac, iPad, Iphone and Apple TV created in collaboration with designer Devine Lu Linvega where you must find your way across an intertwined megastructure. You will not be alone, your incarnations will have the help of bizaroid characters speaking an obscure language.
