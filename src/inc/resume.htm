Skills

I have illustrated many children’s books for edition companies such as Éditions Druide, ERPI, Éditions du Boréal, and Bayard Canada. I have illustrated content for game studios like Edoki Academy, Hibernum Créations, Sava Transmédia, and Japan Studio. I've also done work for animation/TV studios like Toneplus Animation(JP), Mandarine 24, Volta, Pascal Blais Studio, and Télé-Québec. My self-published game {Oquonie} was a finalist for “Excellence in visual art” at the 2015 Independent Games Festival (IGF).
