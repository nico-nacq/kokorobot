Skills

I have illustrated many children’s books for edition companies such as Éditions Druide, ERPI, Éditions du Boréal and Bayard Canada. I have illustrated content for independent and AAA studios. My self-published game Oquonie was a finalist for “Excellence in visual art” at the 2015 Independent Games Festival (IGF). I am an avid cook, and an offshore sailor.
