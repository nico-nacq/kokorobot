Mon ami bulle

Bulle, Tartine, Momo and other characters of the Mon ami Bulle app, travel and make discoveries in unique universes. This project was contract work for the IOS app Mon ami Bulle by Télé Québec.

I worked on many assets for the animated sequences, and did the artwork for 3 of the main narrated stories of the game. The stories were my main task. The project was published in 2016, I worked on the project in 2015.

It was a really lovely project, made with love by an amazing team, but I fear that this format will mean that Mon Ami Bulle will succumb to bit rot. I hope I am wrong.
