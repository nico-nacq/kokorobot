The Solomon's Devils are a group of activists whose goal is to protect the Taiga by way of disrupting any new development, or logging operations in the area.

The group lives in the Taiga full time. Their main camp is nicknamed Hemlock.

The Solomon's Devils was started by {Yegon} and {Kaddali}. Its current members are: {Dae}, {Ikkard}, {Yenifer}, {Anuk}, and occasionally, {Sy} and {Kim}.
