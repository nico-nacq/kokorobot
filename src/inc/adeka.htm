Adeka

  • Story: mindbird
  • Appearance: No visible arms, legs of any kind, just a long serpent-like body with a face that some would argue to be both beautiful and horrifying.
  • Relation: Tormenter of Cee

Adeka is feared by many who share Cee's anxieties. She is a creature most fearsome, that draws you in when you're at your lowest of lows, and refuses to let go. It is possible to escape her grip, but not without losing something important. Adeka will stop at nothing to keep you in her world.
