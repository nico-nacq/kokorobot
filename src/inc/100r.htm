Hundredrabbits

Rek is a writer and illustrator, Devine is a programmer and musician, together we operate a small research lab aboard a sailboat called Hundred Rabbits, where we document, and make experiments on resilience and self-reliance using low-tech solutions.

Visit 100r.co.

Our work focuses on exploring and documenting alternative ways to store power and minimum viable solutions for technological tooling.

Since 2016, we have sailed from Canada, the US West coast, down to Mexico, over to French Polynesia, Cook Islands, Niue, Tonga, New Zealand, Fiji, The Marshall Islands, all the way to Japan and then back again to Canada.
