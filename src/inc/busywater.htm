Busy Water

Busy Water is a game of logic and reasoning, involving a fish named Archie. The player must re-arrange pipes and solve puzzles to help him return home.

  • Publisher Edoki Academy
  • Illustrator Me & Remi Milleret

I illustrated the cut scenes, game logo and intro, most of the puzzles assets (except some of the backgrounds) as well as the fish and cat. I did not make the UI.

Visit Edoki Academy for more information about the game.
