Wunderland Rabbits

A photo project of the rabbits Rek and Dev touring Vancouver Island with their good friends, Kay and Rik.

Rek & Dev never have photos of themselves together, but Kay sends photos after every trip. It is now tradition for all of Kay's pics to become rabbit tour pics.

So far, the rabbits have travelled to Secret Island, the Odgen Point Breakwater, the top of Mount Pkols, Witty's Lagoon and the Victoria Inner Harbour.
