Kokorobot is a mix of kokoro (heart, in Japanese) and robot to mean robot heart. It is meant to have a French pronunciation. It dates back to 2011, see the former logo[95.4 kB] for the website.

The author Rek lives aboard a sailboat somewhere in the Pacific Ocean.

See what they are reading right now, and what tools they are using. Rekka is a name that they chose, inspired from the name Rakka in the anime Haibane Renmei.
