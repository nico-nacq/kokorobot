Shiba Killy

  • Story: mindbird
  • Appearance: Sandy skin, with lighter patches. Never opens his eyes, always has a composed expression and smiles a whole lot. Wears a white jacke with red sleeves, dark jeans and light sneakers.
  • Relation: Friend of Cee

Shiba Killy is a genial hound, a stoic, and the cure to Cee's anxieties. Killy is outgoing, and never fails to bring Cee along, and to protect her. He often puts her into situations that are difficult, he is her link to society, her bridge to the world.

Killy likes pickled foods and to wear ridiculously long scarves.
