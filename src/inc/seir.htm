Seir

  • Alignment: -
  • Gender: Cis male
  • Orientation: Pansexual
  • Appearance: He is very tall, broad-shouldered, thin of waist and strong of body. Hair is dark, with a vivid blue hue. Lines on the face and body. Seir has sharp ears, and dark eyes.

Seir(Cee-urr) is the current head of the {Ars Goetia}, an organization that defends the interests of Hegata people. He communicates with the Solomon's Devils in secret, using {Dae} has a messenger. He is also the largest shareholder of Lesser Key, a private equity firm that is one the largest single owner of real estate in Montore.

Seir believes that in order to make the change Hegatas want in the world that they have to change human minds, and this translates into getting involved with humans on their terms, to speak 'their' language. Seir plays by their rules, amassing enough money and power to buy land in the Taiga to ensure their protection, and future governance.

Seir has worked hard to become infuential in Montore, to the supposed benefit of his people, but he is blind to many things happening around him. Seir is often accused of using his activism as a platform to enrich himself.
